Privacy Policy for Context Keeper

Last updated: August 31, 2025

Overview

Context Keeper is a Chrome extension that helps users save, organize, and retrieve their browsing context. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Collection and Usage

  • Local Data: Notes, sessions, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Optional Sync: If you log in with Supabase, your data may be stored securely in your personal Supabase account.
  • License Keys: If you purchase a premium license via Gumroad, your license key is stored to verify access.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No browsing history is collected.
  • No personal communications (emails, messages, etc.) are collected.
  • No financial or payment data is collected. Purchases are handled externally by Gumroad.
  • No health or sensitive data is collected.

Third-Party Services

  • Gumroad: Used for payment processing and license management. Their privacy policy applies to purchases.
  • Supabase: Used optionally for secure cloud sync. Your data belongs to you.
